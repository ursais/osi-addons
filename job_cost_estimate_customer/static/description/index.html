Sales Job Estimate with Material / Labour / Overhead

Sales Job Estimation to Customer Odoo App

This app allows your sales team to send sales estimates to your customers for materials, labour, and overhead details in job estimation.

Main Features:

  • Salesperson / Sales Manager can create job sales estimation for customers.
  • Salesperson / Sales Manager can add material lines on sales job estimation(s).
  • Salesperson / Sales Manager can add labour lines on sales job estimation(s).
  • Salesperson / Sales Manager can add overhead lines on sales job estimation(s).
  • Project selection on job estimation form as shown.
  • Allow your user to create a sales quotation from the job estimation form view directly.
  • Allow your sales team to send an email to the customer for job estimation(s). It will allow you to print and send by email of the estimate to your customer.
  • Keeps sales estimate reference on sales quotation for reference purposes.
  • For more details please check the below screenshots and watch the video.
